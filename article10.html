Tomcat 基础

一，下载与安装

1，官网下载：wget http://mirrors.tuna.tsinghua.edu.cn/apache/tomcat/tomcat-8/v8.5.12/bin/apache-tomcat-8.5.12.tar.gz

2，校验：md5sum apache-tomcat-8.5.12.tar.gz，看看显示的md5是不是与官方的一样

3，解压：tar zxvf apache-tomcat-8.5.12.tar.gz

4，移动：mv apache-tomcat-8.5.12/* /home/Tomcat/


二，配置最简洁的server.xml

<?xml version='1.0' encoding='utf-8'?>

<Server port="8005" shutdown="SHUTDOWN">

    <Listener className="org.apache.catalina.core.AprLifecycleListener" SSLEngine="on" />

    <Service name="Catalina">

        <Connector port="8080" protocol="HTTP/1.1" connectionTimeout="20000" redirectPort="8443" URIEncoding="UTF-8" />

        <Engine name="Catalina" defaultHost="localhost">

            <Host name="localhost" appBase="" unpackWARs="true" autoDeploy="true">

                <Context path="" docBase="/home/Workspace/project/WebContent" reloadable="false" />

            </Host>

        </Engine>

    </Service>

</Server>


三，启动服务

1，临时启动：/home/Tomcat/bin/startup.sh

2，作为服务启动：nohup /home/Tomcat/bin/startup.sh &

3，重启电脑自动启动：在 /etc/rc.local 中加入：/home/Tomcat/bin/startup.sh


四，IDEA 中配置 启动内存

1，顶部菜单：Run -> Debug Configurations -> VM options 中填写：-Xms2048m -Xmx2048m -XX:MaxNewSize=1024m -XX:MaxPermSize=1024m
